Common questions

Yes, it really is that simple.

Do I need to know anything about AI?
Not a thing. There are no prompts, no sliders, no settings. You pick a photo, tap one of ten plainly-worded actions, and wait about twenty seconds. The AI lives in the background — you never have to talk to it.
What kinds of photos can it mend?
Printed photographs of any age — Polaroids, professional studio portraits, school photos, military ID photos, wedding albums, faded color prints from the 70s and 80s, sepia-toned tintypes, water-damaged photos, photos that have been folded or torn. If you can hold it, snap it, and we can probably mend it.
Will my photos be used to train AI?
No. Your photos are sent encrypted, restored, and returned. They are not retained, not shared, and not used for training — not by us, not by the model providers we use. Sign in with Apple keeps your identity to yourself.
What are the Pro actions?
Pro is a set of three premium effects — Studio, Headshot, and Golden Hour — that go beyond restoration into genuine artistic enhancement. Unlock them once for $1.99 and they're yours forever on any plan.
Is it really iOS-only?
For now, yes. Mend Memories is built for the iPhone and the iPad. We are watching demand for an Android version — if you'd like one, let us know.
How big are the restored photos?
Restorations come back at the resolution they came in at, or upscaled if you chose that action. Either way, the output is print-ready — most users frame the result at 8×10 or larger without any quality loss.
What happens after the free trial?
Nothing surprising. On day 8, your subscription begins at the plan you chose. Apple sends you a reminder before that happens, and you can cancel any time from Settings → Subscriptions on your iPhone.
